Our Story

From humble beginnings to Namibia's premier cricket academy

Founded on Passion for Cricket

Run Whisperers was founded in 2020 in the heart of Namibia by a group of passionate cricket enthusiasts who believed in nurturing local talent. What started as a small community club with just 15 members has grown into a premier cricket academy with over 120 active players.

Our founders, former national team players and certified coaches, recognized the need for professional cricket development in Namibia. They combined their expertise to create a comprehensive training program that focuses not just on skills, but also on character building and sportsmanship.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of Namibia's leading cricket academies, with our teams competing successfully in national tournaments and international competitions across Africa.
