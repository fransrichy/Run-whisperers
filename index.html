About Run Whisperers

Namibia's Premier Cricket Academy

Run Whisperers was founded in the heart of Namibia by a group of passionate cricket enthusiasts. What started as a local club quickly evolved into a competitive team known for its dedication and sportsmanship.

Today, we are a premier cricket academy dedicated to developing talent, promoting sportsmanship, and providing competitive opportunities both nationally and internationally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

When and where do you train?

Our training sessions are held every Tuesday and Thursday evening at the Windhoek Sports Fields. We focus on skill-building and teamwork.

Do you have junior teams?

Yes! We have junior teams for ages 8-16 with age-appropriate coaching and competitions.

How do I register?

You can register online through our booking page or visit us during training hours for on-site registration.

Which leagues do you play?

We participate in the Namibia Cricket League, regional tournaments, and international competitions.

What equipment do I need?

Basic cricket gear including bat, pads, gloves, and helmet. We provide equipment for beginners during trial sessions.

How can I get involved?

Contact us through our website, visit during training, or attend our monthly open days for newcomers.
