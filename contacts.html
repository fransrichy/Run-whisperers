Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the best way to contact the academy?

For quick responses, use our contact form above or call us at +264 81 123 4567 during business hours. For specific inquiries, email the appropriate department directly.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line at +264 81 999 0000.

Can I visit without an appointment?

Yes, you can visit our academy during business hours. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment for tours or meetings with specific staff members.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have ample free parking available for visitors at our Windhoek Sports Fields location.
